Telephone & Email Links

You can also provide ways to contact the person responsible for maintaining the site or someone to address customer concerns, either via phone or email.

Email Links

To provide an email link, you use "mailto:emailaddress" where emailaddress is the email address to send the message to. Clicking this link will open a new message in the userâ€™s default mail client and insert the appropriate contact email address in the To field.

Telephone Links

To provide a link to a telephone number, use "tel:+1number" where +1 is the international dialing prefix (e.g., +1 is for the US, +61 is for Christmas Island) and number is the phone number to call, including the area code. Tapping this link on your phone will display a dialog box asking if you want to call that number.
